Our Story

Some love stories are written in the stars. Ours began with a click.

On the first day of April 2024, a simple message on Shaadi.com sparked a connection between two worlds—Manthan, a thoughtful and analytical fund manager from Ahmedabad, and Shreya, a vibrant IT professional from the picturesque town of Baramati, working in Pune. What started as a digital introduction soon turned into something much more meaningful, magical, and real.

Despite the 500 kilometers between them, distance never stood a chance...

Manthan, who usually dealt with market graphs and numbers, began charting a new course—traveling frequently, sometimes twice a month for 10 days, from Ahmedabad to Pune. Each trip was not just a journey across cities, but steps toward a deeper understanding, heartfelt conversations, and countless shared smiles...

Among all their moments, one special gift from Shreya truly touched Manthan's heart—a beautifully crafted frame with a photo of one of their most cherished memories...

And just when Shreya thought she knew all of Manthan’s sides, he left everyone stunned at their engagement on 14th June 2025 in Baramati...

Now, as they prepare to say “I do” on 15th November 2025 in Ahmedabad, they invite you to celebrate the union of two hearts that met unexpectedly, stayed intentionally, and chose each other wholeheartedly...
